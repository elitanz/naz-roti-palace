Cook fresh, cook with love

A taste of Trinidad,
right here in Miramar

Every roti is rolled and cooked fresh to order — soft, layered buss-up-shut and dalpuri that comes out warm and ready to soak up the curry.

The curry is rich but never too salty. The goat is slow-cooked and tender. And the doubles? Get there early, because the regulars know — they go fast. It's the food that reminds Trini families of home, made the proper way by people who care how it tastes.

Yes, there may be a little wait while it's all made fresh. Everyone agrees: it's worth it. That's how Naz Roti Palace earned 4.8 stars and a crowd that keeps coming back.
